My Personal Interests

One of my greatest joys in life is rock climbing. I really enjoy being up in the mountains
where I can feel the cool breeze on my back and seeing the world from hundreds of feet in the
air. Being up there only hearing the birds and the breeze and feeling the rock beneath your
fingers and the rope pulling at your harness truly is a different experience.

Second thing I really enjoy is school. I love to learn new things and try out other concepts. I
have only done a little bit of school but have enjoyed it a lot. When I started school I started
as a Biology Major, but then I switched to Computer Science after taking one class and ejoying it
so much that I now feel that's what I want to do with my life. I still hope to learn about lots of
different concept throughout my life as well though.

Lastly, I LOVE pickleball. I started playing pretty recently, and I have already been able to compete
in several tournaments and I have performed pretty well. If I'm being honest I could probably play pickleball
for several hours every day and still not even be phased. My passion for pickleball probably stems from my
love of sports. I could play any sport and enjoy it, with maybe the exception of golf.
